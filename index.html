Welcome & Consent
Please read before continuing

This study tests whether a personalized disease prevention plan — built from your actual genetic risk variants and lifestyle inputs — is more useful than standard population-average health guidelines. You will enter your own data, receive an AI-generated plan tailored specifically to you, compare it against generic advice, and rate both sets on five criteria. All responses are completely anonymous. No personal health data is stored anywhere. This is a 9th grade science fair research study for the 2026 Future Science Innovators competition.

⚠️ Educational only. GenomeGuide does not provide medical advice. All recommendations are for research evaluation purposes only. Please consult a qualified physician for any real health decisions.
