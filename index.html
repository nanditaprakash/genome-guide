Welcome & Consent
Please read before continuing

This study tests whether a personalized disease prevention plan — based on your genetic risk factors and lifestyle — is more useful than standard population-average guidelines. You'll enter your own data, receive a plan tailored to you, compare it to generic advice, and rate both. All responses are anonymous. No personal health information is stored or transmitted anywhere. This is a 9th grade science fair study for the 2026 Future Science Innovators competition.

⚠️ Educational only. GenomeGuide does not provide medical advice. Recommendations are for research evaluation purposes only. Consult a physician for real health guidance.
